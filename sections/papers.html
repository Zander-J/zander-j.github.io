Annual Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS 2025)
Plug-and-play Feature Causality Decomposition for Multimodal Representation Learning
Ye Liu, Zihan Ji, Hongmin Cai#

[OpenReview]

Proposes a plug-and-play causal feature decomposition module for multimodal learning, disentangling unimodal features into modality-invariant, unique, and redundant parts; removes aleatoric uncertainty via backdoor adjustment while preserving complementary information for fusion.

AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI 2025 Oral)

AFFAKT: A Hierarchical Optimal Transport Based Method for Affective Facial Knowledge Transfer in Video Deception Detection
Zihan Ji, Xuetao Tian, Ye Liu#
[Paper] [arXiv] [Code]

Proposes AFFAKT, a knowledge transfer method from facial expression datasets to video deception detection. Uses H-OTKT for optimal relation mapping and SRKB for invariant correlation retention, with sample-specific re-weighting at inference.

Chinese Conference on Pattern Recognition and Computer Vision (PRCV 2024)

OCR-Aware Scene Graph Generation via Multi-modal Object Representation Enhancement and Logical Bias Learning
Xinyu Zhou*, Zihan Ji*, Anna Zhu#
[Paper]

Proposes OCR-aware Scene Graph Generation (OSGG) by incorporating scene text into SGG, with causal inference-based debiasing and cross-modal visual feature enhancement; achieves state-of-the-art on TextCaps-SG and VG, excelling in cross-modal retrieval.
