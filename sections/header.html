Zihan JI 冀子晗

I am now a 1st-year PhD student at College of Control Science and Engineering (CSE) in Zhejiang University (ZJU) under the supervision of Prof. Qi YE. My research interests include World Action Model (WAM), Visuo-Tactile Representation Modeling etc. for manipulation. My goal is to empower robots with the ability to understand and interact with the world through multimodal perception and reasoning, enabling them to perform complex tasks in unstructured environments.
Before pursuing my PhD, I was a master's student at the School of Future Technology in South China University of Technology (SCUT), working on multimodal representation learning, affective computing, transfer learning etc., supervised by Prof. Ye LIU and Prof. Xuetao TIAN (from Beijing Normal University). During my master's studies, I finished internship visits at Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU) and TCL, working on the topic of LLM/Agents applications.
I achieved my bachelor's degree from Wuhan University of Technology (WHUT) in 2023, majoring in Artificial Intelligence. During my undergraduate studies, I received guidance from Prof. Anna ZHU and Prof. Xing LIU.
